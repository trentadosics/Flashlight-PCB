[EN]  

Components:  
- 3 transparent LEDS  
- 1 push button  
- 1 3VCR2032 battery holder  
- 1 3VCR2032 battery  

Tools:
- 1 Solder  
- Tin 0.5mm diameter
- Metallic sponge to clean the tip of the solder
  
How to solder:  
3 steps of 3 seconds each  
Previously, excepte for the battery holder, place all the components on the PCB  
1. Heat the zone to be solded  
2. Bring closer the tin to the heated area to melt it between the OCB and the component. It will work as a glue between them.  
3. Take out the tin and leave the the solder 3 more seconds  

Step by step:  
SOLDER BATTERY HOLDER  
- Solder one pad of the battery holder  
- Melt the solded tin and place the battery holder  
- Melt tin over the other battery holder's terminal
  
SOLDER PUSH BUTTON  
- Place the push button  
- Melt some tin over each terminal
  
SOLDER LEDS  
- Place each LED (remember: the long leg is the positive terminal!!)  
- Molt some tin over each terminal
